QuickLinks App

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

This application stores links locally on the user's device.
No personal information is collected, requested, transmitted, or stored. This includes, but is not limited to, names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, identifiers, or usage profiles.

The application does not require user accounts.
The QuickLinks app is device-specific and operates without registration.

When a user chooses to send a link to another device, a mutual handshake and explicit consent between the sending and receiving devices is required before any transfer can occur.
Devices are identified only by server-defined nicknames.
No personal data is associated with these nicknames.

Links sent between devices may temporarily pass through a server solely for delivery purposes. If a link cannot be delivered within 48 hours, it is automatically deleted.
No links or metadata are stored beyond this delivery window.

The application does not track users, does not use analytics, does not display ads, and does not share any data with third parties.

All data remains private, personal, discrete and under the control of the user.
